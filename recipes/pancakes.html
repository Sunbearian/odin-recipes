Pancakes

A quick and simple pancake recipe

This pancake recipe is a fool proof crepe recipe that insures perfect pancakes every time. Does not guarantee a good and successful flip!

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 table spoon or sunflower or vegetable oil, plus a little extra for frying
  • Lemon/ sugar or other filling as preferred

Steps

  1. Put the flour, eggs, milk and oil into a mixing bowl and whisk to a smooth batter
  2. Set aside for 30 mins to rest if you have time. Can use straight away
  3. Set frying pan to medium heat and wipe with some oiled kitchen paper
  4. Once the frying pan is hot, cook for 1 min each side until golden
  5. Serve with your preferred filling
