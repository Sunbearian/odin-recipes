Omelette in a Mug

A quick and easy way to make an omelettee when time is short and you don't want to create a load of washing up.

Omelette in a Mug is a great way to cook an omelette with just a mug when time is short. Essentially you make a normal omelette of your choosing but mix it all in a mug and put in the microwave.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of Cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of diced fully cooked ham
  • 1 tablespoon diced bell pepper - colour of your choosing

Steps

  1. Break eggs into greased mug and whisk with a fork
  2. Add the milk and whisk again
  3. Stir in the other ingredients
  4. Microwave the mug on high for 30 seconds and stir
  5. Microwave again for another 30 seconds. stir and repeat depending on Microwave
