Honey Mustard Chicken

A tangy and delicious grilled chicken recipe.

This honey mustard chicken recipe is quick and simple to make and is a great way to spice up some chicken breasts.

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
  • 4 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon steak sauce
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill for medium heat.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mix the mustard, honey, mayonnaise, and steak sauce. Set a small amount for basting the chicken later and dip the remaining chicken into the remaining sauce to coat.
  3. Lightly oil the grill. Grill the chicken over indirect heat for 18 to 20 minutes, turning occasionally, or until the juices run clear. Baste occasionally with the reserved sauce during the last 10 minutes. Watch carefully to prevent burning.
